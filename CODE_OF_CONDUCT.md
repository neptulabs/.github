# Code of Conduct

The Neptu / neptulabs projects are dedicated to a respectful, professional, and inclusive community.

By participating in our repositories (issues, discussions, pull requests), you agree to:

- Be respectful of other contributors and users.
- Provide constructive feedback and accept feedback in the same spirit.
- Avoid personal attacks, harassment, and discriminatory language.
- Assume good intent, but acknowledge impact.

Project maintainers may remove or edit contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation for behavior they deem inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact: **conduct@neptu.com**.
